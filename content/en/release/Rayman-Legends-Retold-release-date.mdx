<Callout type="warning" title="Not a 3D Platformer">

</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="The Vision Behind the Remake">
Loïc Gounon, Brand Producer for Rayman Legends Retold, stated that the remake is "our way of revisiting what makes Rayman so special, and re-telling that experience for a new generation of players." The goal was to preserve everything fans loved about the original while expanding Rayman's lore and its world logic. The original levels and visuals were used as a reference point to add details and depth, making the 2D gameplay more vibrant.

The game runs at a solid 60 FPS on Nintendo Switch 2, a testament to the optimization work by Ubisoft, applying lessons learned from projects like the *Star Wars Outlaws* port. This focus on performance ensures a smooth and enjoyable experience across all platforms. The developers aimed for an imaginative remake that distinguishes itself from the original, offering a modern throwback feel.
