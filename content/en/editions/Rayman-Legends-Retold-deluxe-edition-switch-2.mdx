<Section icon={Target} title="New Gameplay Mechanics and the Sixth Realm">

While the core platforming of the 2013 original remains intact, *Retold* introduces several new layers to the gameplay loop. The most significant addition is the **Land of the Livid Dead**, which serves as a brand-new sixth realm. Unlike the original game's Land of the Livid Dead (which was a bonus challenge area), this version is a fully realized world with its own narrative arc, unique mechanics involving magical light, and a climactic boss battle against a new shadowy villain.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="World Navigation">
    Explore the reimagined Glade of Dreams via a new 3D world map that replaces the painting gallery from the original game.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Dragon Riding Sequences">
    Engage in on-rails flight levels that play similarly to Star Fox. These sequences bridge the gap between main worlds and utilize the 3D engine for epic scale.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Teensy Rescue Operations">
    Locate hidden Teensies in both returning and new levels to unlock the Cave of Trials and advanced musical stages.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Rhythm Mastery">

  </Step>

The dragon riding sequences represent the biggest departure from the traditional 2D formula. These levels are designed to be cinematic and forgiving, focusing on the spectacle of flying through the newly rendered 3D environments. Players will navigate through narrow canyons, dodge projectiles, and use Rayman's abilities to clear obstacles while moving at high speeds.

| Mechanic | Description | Primary Goal |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Dragon Riding** | On-rails 3D flight sequences | Travel between worlds and survive |
| **Magical Light** | Mechanic in the 6th Realm | Navigate dark areas and reveal platforms |
| **Cave of Trials** | High-difficulty challenge rooms | Earn exclusive medals and rewards |
| **Kung Foot 2.0** | Improved soccer mini-game | Local and online multiplayer competition |

<Callout type="warning" title="Visual Readability">
Some veteran players may find the transition to 3D polygons slightly changes the "hitbox feel" of platforms. It is recommended to spend some time in the early levels of the Land of the Livid Dead to adjust to the new depth perception.
</Callout>
