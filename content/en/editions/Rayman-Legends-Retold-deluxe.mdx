<Section icon={Sparkles} title="New Gameplay Mechanics in Retold">

The "Retold" aspect of **Rayman Legends Retold deluxe** introduces significant mechanical shifts. The most notable is the transition between traditional side-scrolling and 3D "on-rails" dragon rides. These segments function similarly to Star Fox, where Rayman soars through the Glade of Dreams to reach new worlds, replacing the painting-based hub world of the original game with a dynamic, interconnected world map.

**Key Innovations:**
- **The Sixth Realm**: A completely new world added to the end of the main campaign with unique mechanics.
- **Voiced Cinematics**: Story beats are now delivered through fully voiced cutscenes, providing deeper lore for the Teensies and the Bubble Dreamer.
- **Cave of Trials**: A dedicated mode for daily and weekly challenges with global leaderboards.
- **Improved Kung Foot**: The fan-favorite minigame returns with customizable rules, power-ups, and tighter controls for competitive play.

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Strategy">
Use the new 3D camera tilt to your advantage. In certain levels, the camera will rotate to reveal hidden paths or Teensie cages that were "behind" the scenery in the original 2D version.
</Callout>
