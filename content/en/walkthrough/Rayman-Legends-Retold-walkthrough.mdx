### Dragon Ride Mechanics

These levels are designed to feel like a rollercoaster, offering an immersive and fast-paced experience. You'll need to master several mechanics to succeed.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Flying & Maneuvering">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Shooting & Charged Attacks">
    Utilize fireballs to clear paths or defeat enemies. Charged attacks can deliver more powerful bursts, essential for tougher foes or breaking certain barriers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Dodging & Timing">
    Anticipate incoming projectiles and environmental hazards. Timely dodges are crucial for survival and protecting your precious Lums.

  <Step num="4" title="Spatial Awareness">
