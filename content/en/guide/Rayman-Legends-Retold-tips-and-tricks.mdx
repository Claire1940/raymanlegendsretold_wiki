### Dragon Rides: A New Thrill

The new dragon-riding levels are fast-paced, third-person action sequences that bridge the journey between worlds. These segments are reminiscent of classic rail shooters, offering a dynamic change of pace. Each ride starts with accompanying Teensies, and collisions cause you to lose them, adding an incentive for a no-hit run.

<Callout type="success" title="Dragon Ride Strategy">
During dragon rides, focus on **dodging obstacles** using the roll buttons and prioritizing **powerful charged fireballs** to destroy tough barriers. Saving your Teensies during these segments is crucial for higher scores.
</Callout>

### Invaded Levels and Challenge Rooms

"Invaded" versions of normal levels transform them into time-attack runs, requiring quick reflexes and precise platforming to save more Teensies. These levels are excellent for practicing speedrunning techniques and understanding Rayman's momentum. Additionally, short challenge rooms within levels require clever environmental manipulation, often involving companion characters like Murphy, to save extra Teensies.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat and Co-op Strategies">
While Rayman Legends Retold is primarily a platformer, combat plays a role, and the returning four-player co-op significantly alters gameplay dynamics. The game offers a lighthearted romp with cartoonish goofiness, but effective strategies still matter for success.

### Effective Combat Techniques

Combat in Rayman Legends Retold is straightforward but requires good timing. Rayman's basic punches and kicks are effective against most enemies. For more challenging foes or environmental hazards, precise movement and sometimes specific character abilities become crucial.

<Callout type="warning" title="Beware the Ghost">
In "Invaded" levels, the ghost of Rayman mirrors your movements. Avoid touching it at all costs, as it results in instant death. Planning your route and jumps to stay ahead is essential.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Performance and Visuals">
Rayman Legends Retold boasts a significant visual overhaul, transforming the original's gorgeous 2D visuals into a stunning 2.5D world. This modern graphical sensibility, powered by the Snowdrop engine, gives the game a Dreamworks animated film aesthetic.

### Graphical Enhancements

Ubisoft Montpellier and Ubisoft Milan have painstakingly recreated the game, adding details that give extra depth to its 2D gameplay.
