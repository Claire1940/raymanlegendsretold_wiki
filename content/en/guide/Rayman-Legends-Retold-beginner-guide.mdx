<Section icon={Target} title="Mastering Core Mechanics and Controls">
At its core, Rayman Legends Retold is a 2D action platformer that demands precise movement and quick reflexes. Understanding Rayman's abilities and how they interact with the environment is key to success, especially in the more challenging "Invaded" levels.

<Callout type="warning" title="Momentum is Key">
In "Invaded" levels, which are time-attack runs, mastering Rayman's momentum is crucial. Learn the timing of each jump and attack to maintain speed and bypass obstacles efficiently. Mistakes can be frustrating, but they offer clear lessons for improvement.
</Callout>

Here's a breakdown of essential moves and their applications:

| Action         | Description                                        | Primary Use Case                                    | Advanced Tip                                          |
| :------------- | :------------------------------------------------- | :-------------------------------------------------- | :---------------------------------------------------- |
| **Run**        | Standard horizontal movement.                      | Traversing levels, building speed.                  | Combine with jumps for longer distances.               |
| **Jump**       | Basic vertical leap.                               | Clearing gaps, reaching higher platforms.           | Vary jump height by holding the button.               |
| **Wall-Jump**  | Jumping off walls to gain height.                  | Ascending vertical shafts, bypassing obstacles.     | String multiple wall-jumps for rapid vertical ascent. |
| **Punch/Kick** | Rayman's basic attack.                             | Defeating enemies, breaking certain barriers.       | Use mid-air attacks for extended reach.               |
| **Helicopter Hair** | Slows descent by spinning Rayman's hair.           | Extending jumps, gliding over hazards, precise landings. | Essential for navigating updrafts and thorny vines.    |
| **Swing on Vines** | Grabbing and swinging from hanging elements.       | Crossing large gaps, gaining momentum for jumps.    | Release at peak swing for maximum distance.           |

The game's controls are highly responsive, ensuring that every input translates directly to Rayman's actions. Practice these moves to build muscle memory and navigate the diverse levels with confidence.

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Saving Teensies and Progression">
Teensies are the blue, freaky inhabitants of the world, and saving them is your primary objective. Collecting enough Teensies unlocks new levels and zones, driving your progression through the game.

<Callout type="info" title="Teensy Collection Strategy">
Teensies are often hidden in plain sight, but many require specific platforming sequences or solving environmental puzzles. Don't rush through levels; take your time to explore every nook and cranny. Replaying levels, especially "Invaded" versions, is often necessary to collect all of them.
</Callout>
