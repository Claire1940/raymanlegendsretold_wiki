<Section icon={Swords} title="Multiplayer Dynamics and Co-op Chaos">

The interaction between **Rayman Legends Retold characters** shines brightest in the local co-op mode. Supporting up to four players on a single screen, the game encourages a mix of cooperation and lighthearted competition. Players can work together to reach high-up Teensies or race each other to collect the most Lums in a stage.

The Kung Foot mode also returns with significant improvements. This mini-game allows players to use their chosen characters in a fast-paced soccer-style match. With improved controls and new power-ups, Kung Foot remains the perfect break from the main adventure.

| Co-op Role | Description | Benefit | Shared Chaos Level |
