<Section icon={Sparkles} title="What is Rayman Legends Retold? A Modern Reimagining">

Rayman Legends Retold is a comprehensive 3D remake of the critically acclaimed 2013 platformer, *Rayman Legends*. This new iteration breathes fresh life into the beloved classic, offering players a visually stunning and narratively enriched experience. While it transforms the original's crisp 2D art into dynamic 3D environments, the core 2D platforming action remains intact, providing a familiar yet fresh feel. The game is built using the Snowdrop engine, known for its exquisite lighting and textures, previously seen in titles like *The Division 2* and *Avatar: Frontiers of Pandora*.

<Callout type="tip">
The development teams at Ubisoft Milan and Ubisoft Montpellier spent three years crafting Rayman Legends Retold, laying the groundwork for potential future Rayman adventures.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Enhanced Features and Gameplay Innovations">

Rayman Legends Retold introduces a wealth of new features and gameplay enhancements that elevate the original experience. Beyond the visual overhaul, players can expect an expanded soundtrack by Christophe Héral and Grant Kirkhope, alongside fully voiced cinematics and characters—a first for the franchise. This brings the journey to life in new and exciting ways, adding depth to the beloved cast.

<Callout type="success" title="New Content Alert">

Gameplay sees significant improvements, blending the visual richness of *Rayman 2* and *3* with the clarity of *Rayman Legends*' side-scrolling action. The game offers a rich selection of returning levels, meticulously recreated in 3D, ensuring that fans of the original will find plenty to love while experiencing it anew.

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Multiplayer and Challenge Modes">

The adventure in Rayman Legends Retold can be a shared experience, with support for up to four players in couch co-op. This adds a new layer of chaotic fun, allowing friends to rescue each other, race for Lums, or even playfully sabotage each other to claim Teensies. The popular mini-game, Kung Foot, also makes a return with improved controls, power-ups, and customizable rules, promising even more competitive fun.

<Callout type="warning" title="Challenge Ahead">
The Cave of Trials offers ongoing free challenges, pushing players' skills to the limit in a series of difficult tests.
</Callout>

For those seeking to test their mettle, the game provides a dedicated "Cave of Trials" offering ongoing free challenges. These trials are designed to push players' platforming abilities to their absolute maximum, providing endless replayability and opportunities for skill mastery.
