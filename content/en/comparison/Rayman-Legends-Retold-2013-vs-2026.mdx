<Section icon={Shield} title="Development and Vision">
**Rayman Legends Retold** is a collaborative effort between Ubisoft Montpelier and Ubisoft Milan. The development team's goal is to **revisit what makes Rayman special** and re-tell that experience for a new generation of players, while preserving everything fans love from the 2013 original. Brand Producer Loïc Gounon emphasizes that *Legends* provided the perfect foundation to expand Rayman's lore, its world logic, and how everything connects.

The developers have taken a thoughtful design approach to updating the visuals, ensuring that the 3D rendition still captures the whimsical charm of the 2D art style. They've even shown how the 2D engine runs under the 3D visuals, using unique visual tricks to achieve a 2.5D feel, making the gameplay feel identical to the original despite the visual changes. This dedication ensures that the **Rayman Legends Retold 2013 vs 2026** comparison ultimately highlights evolution rather than a complete departure.
