<Section icon={Target} title="Developer Insights and Future of Rayman">
The development of **Rayman Legends Retold PS4** has been a collaborative effort between Ubisoft Montpelier and Ubisoft Milan, taking three years to bring this ambitious remake to life. Ubisoft Montpelier focused on integrating the original UbiArt tools within the Snowdrop engine, allowing them to leverage existing assets while adding new 3D depth. Ubisoft Milan, known for their work on the Mario + Rabbids games, handled character modeling and animations, ensuring a fresh yet familiar look for the iconic cast.

Ubisoft has hinted that this remake could be a stepping stone for future Rayman games. The experience and tools developed for Retold might very well be carried forward into new, unconfirmed Rayman adventures. This signals a potential resurgence for the limbless hero, exciting fans who have waited over a decade for a new mainline title.
