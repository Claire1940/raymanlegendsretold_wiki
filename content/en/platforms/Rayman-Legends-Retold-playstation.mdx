<Section icon={Map} title="Visual and Gameplay Overhaul">

The most striking change in **Rayman Legends Retold playstation** is the move to the Snowdrop Engine. This transition provides higher fidelity and more complex lighting effects than the original UbiArt Framework. Brand Art Director Guillame Arvieu describes this approach as a "bridge" between the visual richness of 3D titles like Rayman 2 and the mechanical clarity of side-scrolling platformers.

| Feature | Original (2013) | Retold (2026) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Engine** | UbiArt Framework | Snowdrop Engine |
| **Art Style** | 2D Painterly / Pre-rendered | Full 3D Environments |
| **Dialogue** | Gibberish / Text Bubbles | Fully Voiced Cinematics |
| **Framerate** | 60 FPS (Most Platforms) | 60 FPS (Locked on PS5/Switch 2) |
| **Perspective** | Fixed 2D Side-scrolling | Dynamic 2D Action in 3D Space |

The gameplay loop still revolves around rescuing Teensies, but the "Retold" aspect introduces narrative twists. A shadowy new villain is corrupting the Glade of Dreams, forcing Rayman and his friends—Globox, Barbara, and Grand Minimus—to navigate reimagined versions of classic stages.

<Callout type="info" title="Environmental Interaction">
Players will still use Murphy, the green bottle fly, to manipulate the environment. On PlayStation 5, the DualSense controller's haptic feedback and adaptive triggers add a tactile layer to rotating wheels or cutting ropes for Rayman.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Multiplayer and Kung Foot">

Multiplayer remains a cornerstone of the experience. **Rayman Legends Retold playstation** supports four-player couch co-op, allowing friends to jump into any level at any time. This leads to "fun chaos" where players can help each other reach high platforms or accidentally knock each other into hazards while racing for Lums.

The fan-favorite mini-game, **Kung Foot**, has also been significantly upgraded. This 2D soccer combat mode now features:
- **Customizable Rules**: Toggle gravity settings or goal-switching.
- **Power-ups**: Random drops like gold shields or speed boosts.
- **Platform Layouts**: Multiple arena designs to change the flow of the match.

| Mode | Player Count | Style |

| **Kung Foot** | 2-4 Players | Competitive Soccer Combat |
