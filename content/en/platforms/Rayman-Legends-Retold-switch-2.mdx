<Section icon={Map} title="Enhanced Gameplay & Visuals on Switch 2">
**Rayman Legends Retold** on Switch 2 is not just a visual upgrade; it's an expansion of the beloved platforming experience. The game brings the classic 2D action into a vibrant 3D world, featuring entirely reworked visuals powered by the Snowdrop engine. This allows for higher fidelity, adding depth to the familiar levels and introducing spectacular new environments. The Switch 2 version is confirmed to maintain a solid 60 frames per second, a testament to the optimization efforts by Ubisoft, drawing lessons from ports like Star Wars Outlaws.

The reimagined world includes new character interactions and surprises around every corner, making the Glade of Dreams more immersive than ever. The unifying story is presented through fully voiced cinematics, adding significant context and meaning to the adventure, a notable departure from the original's gallery-style world progression.
