### Enhanced Gameplay Elements

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Dynamic Environments">
    Levels now feature more interactive elements and environmental hazards, requiring precise timing and quick reflexes. The Snowdrop engine allows for higher fidelity, bringing more depth to the 2D gameplay.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Invaded Levels Overhaul">
    "Invaded" levels, which are time-attack runs, have been refined to challenge players even more. Understanding Rayman's momentum and the timing of each jump is crucial for saving Teensies under strict time limits.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="New Power-ups and Abilities">
    While not fundamentally changing the core move set, new power-ups and character interactions offer fresh ways to navigate obstacles and defeat enemies. Expect surprises around every corner.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Co-op Experience">
    The return of four-player co-op ensures that **Rayman Legends Retold** remains a fantastic shared experience. This feature, not common in many modern platformers, adds a unique social dimension to the game.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The game's variety is a major highlight, with levels like the Stinkbog introducing updraft mechanics that force players to control Rayman's helicopter hair while dodging thorny vines. These diverse challenges prevent the experience from becoming repetitive, keeping players engaged throughout their journey to restore peace to the Glade of Dreams.

</Section>
